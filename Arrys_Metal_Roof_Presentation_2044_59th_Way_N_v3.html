What makes a roof FORTIFIED

  • i.A sealed roof deck — even if wind strips the covering, the sealed deck keeps most water out. This is the exact failure Pinellas saw 40,000 times in 2024.
  • ii.Stronger attachment, engineered edges — enhanced fastening and locked-down edges at the roof's most vulnerable points, stricter than code.
  • iii.Independent verification — an IBHS-certified evaluator (not us) inspects the work. Your certificate is proof, not promises.
  • iv.Real leverage in Florida — documents wind-mitigation features for insurance discounts; upgrades can qualify for My Safe Florida Home grants (up to $10,000) and PACE financing.

Why this is the Smiths' edge

When nearly every roof in Tyrone Garden Manor was built to minimum code, a FORTIFIED certificate makes yours provably different — to your insurer, to a future buyer, and to the next storm with a name.

Questions your neighbors ask us

Is a metal roof worth the higher upfront cost?
Over its life, usually yes. One metal roof can outlast two to three shingle roofs while cutting cooling bills and often earning insurance credits. Ask us to walk through total cost of ownership — and about financing and payment options.
How long does installation take?
Most residential metal re-roofs finish in a matter of days once materials arrive. We handle permits, protect landscaping, clean up daily, and finish with a magnetic sweep.
Will it help with my homeowner's insurance?
In most cases, yes — and after 2024, Pinellas carriers scrutinize roofs harder than ever. A wind-rated metal system documented on Florida's wind-mitigation form frequently earns 15–35% premium credits. We provide the documentation.
What maintenance does a metal roof need?
Very little — occasional visual checks and keeping debris out of valleys and gutters. No re-nailing, no granule loss, no curling.
