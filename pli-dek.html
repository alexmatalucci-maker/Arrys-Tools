Waterproof Walking Deck System · Technical Overview

The Pli-Dek®
deck system.

A cementitious waterproof walking-deck system for wood-framed decks. Unlike vinyl membranes or traffic coatings, Pli-Dek builds a durable, cement-based walking surface that protects the structure below from water intrusion — engineered for pedestrian traffic on elevated balconies, breezeways, roof decks, and stairs.

One · How the system is built

Ten layers,
a fraction of an inch thick.

Pli-Dek is a monolithic, reinforced assembly built up in stages over an exterior plywood substrate. The finished system is only a fraction of an inch thick — yet it delivers full waterproofing and a finished walking surface in one continuous build-up.

  • Exterior plywood substrate — the structural deck
  • Pli-Dek seam paper · covers plywood joints
  • Flashing system · walls, doors, posts, penetrations
  • Galvanized metal lath · G60 hot-dipped, fully embedded
  • Polymer cement base coat — encapsulates the lath
  • Waterproof acrylic membrane
  • Intermediate coat
  • Texture coat · smooth to heavy
  • Color coat
  • Optional clear sealer · added protection

Galvanized metal lath fully embedded in a polymer-modified cement base coat creates one continuous reinforced surface — the foundation of Pli-Dek's monolithic performance.

Four · The monolithic advantage

No cement backer board,
no board joints to fail.

A primary advantage of Pli-Dek is that it does not require a cement backer board (such as Securock® or other cementitious boards). Instead of many mechanically-fastened panels, the galvanized lath is embedded in polymer-modified cement to form one continuous reinforced surface — so the deck performs as a single unit.

Pli-Dek Monolithic SystemCement Backer-Board Systems
ReinforcementGalvanized lath embedded in cement — continuousIndividual boards, mechanically fastened
JointsNo board seamsMultiple panel joints
Crack riskReduced — no seams to telegraphJoints can telegraph & crack with movement
BondCement encapsulates the lathRelies on fastened boards
ComponentsFewer parts & transitionsMore components & failure points
InstallNo board install — less material & timeAdds board material & labor

Because there are no cement-board seams, the finished surface is less susceptible to cracks from panel expansion, contraction, or differential movement — letting the whole deck assembly perform as one continuous unit and helping minimize joint-related maintenance.

Installation requirements

  • ✦Manufacturer-trained, approved applicators only — Pli-Dek must be installed by certified installers.
  • ✦Proper plywood joint gapping & seam treatment — joints require correct gapping and seam paper.
  • ✦Critical flashing at walls, doors, posts, scuppers, and all penetrations.
  • ✦Manufacturer installation instructions must be available on the jobsite.
